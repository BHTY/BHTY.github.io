Additional Info

1 manufacturing point = 8 damage points repaired on any ship (shield/weapon = 2 points)

Ships can only be built at shipyards, repaired at shipyards or starbases

 

Turn Phases

  1. Manufacturing / Repair Phase
    1. Each player may choose to allot some of their industrial points for that turn to build or repair any ships. Any ships being repaired or built will not be available for movement until the next turn. If a sector with ships being built comes under attack later in the turn, they will not be available, and those under repair will be in their non-repaired condition. Any industrial points unused can be carried forward into future turns.
    2. Note that an unlimited number of ships can be repaired each turn (provided that you have sufficient industrial points available), but only one ship can be produced from each shipyard per turn.
  2. Movement Phase
    1. A player can move any of their ships any number of hexes up to their maximum warp (their standard max speed multiplied by the percentage of warp power available), but they cannot warp through sectors with enemy vessels/stations in them – they must drop out of warp to fight.
